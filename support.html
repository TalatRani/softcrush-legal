Account help

  • If you forgot your password, use the password reset option on the sign-in screen.
  • If you need help accessing your account, contact us from the email linked to your account.
  • You can manage or delete your account from inside the app.

Safety and reporting

If someone behaves inappropriately, use the block or report tools inside the app. Reports are reviewed by our team.

For urgent danger or emergencies, contact local emergency services immediately.
